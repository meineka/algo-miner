## Key findings (extracted into research/aziz/knowledge.md)

### Capital & timing
- Minimum starting capital: **10 000 USD** (his recommendation, not the US PDT 25k floor)
- Volatility window: **09:30 – 11:00 New York** is where most price action happens
- He personally does not trade afternoon sessions

### What he trades
- Preference order: **Stocks & ETFs > Options > Crypto > Forex**
- Reasons:
  - Stocks: simple, always volatile on news, market hours bounded
  - Crypto: 24/7, "not healthy", unregulated, no structure
  - Forex: only 11 main pairs, sub-hour timeframes are noise
- During elevated market-wide volatility (e.g. 2020 pandemic) he switches
  to ETFs and indexes (SPY, QQQ, SPXL, TQQQ).

### Stocks-in-Play scanner (re-confirmed)
- Gap > **2 %** up or down
- Premarket volume ≥ **100 000 shares** (transcript OCR garbled but
  this matches his book)
- Float ≥ **20 M shares** (he avoids penny stocks under 20 M float)
- Price ≥ **10 USD**

### Float categories (re-confirmed verbatim quote)
> "Am liebsten mag ich Aktien mit mittlerem Streubesitz […]
> 10 – 100 USD […] Aktien mit hohem und mittleren Streubesitz […]
> [Penny Stocks] meide ich"

### News catalysts ranked
1. Earnings (biggest)
2. FDA approvals (pharma)
3. M&A (e.g. Musk × Twitter)
4. Contract wins/losses
5. Layoffs / management changes
6. Stock splits

### Trading station setup
- **6 monitors** in his home office, 3 portable when traveling
- One monitor each for: 1-min chart, 5-min/daily chart, Level 2,
  scanners, chatroom, watchlist
- Indicators on chart: **9 EMA + 20 EMA** (exponential),
  **50 SMA + 200 SMA** (simple), **VWAP** (favourite),
  **prior-day close**, **Camarilla pivots**
- *"Use indicators sparingly — no magic numbers."*

### Broker / platform
- Broker: **Interactive Brokers** (also Cobra Trading, Capital Markets, TD Ameritrade for active trading)
- Platform: **DAS Trader Pro** (direct-access, millisecond execution)
- Avoid commission-free retail brokers (Robinhood): *"like bringing a plastic knife to a gunfight"*
- News feed: **Benzinga Pro** (free tier)
- Earnings calendar: **tradingterminal.com**

### Strategies covered in this video (4 main + ad-hoc reversal)
1. **Opening Range Breakout (ORB)** — his most-used
   - Wait first 5 minutes (or 1, 15, 30 depending on style)
   - Long on close above range high (gap-up stocks), short on close below range low (gap-down stocks)
   - Stop below VWAP
2. **Bull Flag** — strong impulse + consolidation flag + breakout w/ volume
3. **ABCD pattern** — same shape as Bull Flag but for higher-priced stocks
4. **9/20 Reversal** ("920") — counter-trend, invented by **Brian** (likely Brian Shannon)
   - 2-min chart only
   - Stock strongly up between 09:30 – 10:00
   - Pull-back to 20 EMA between 10:00 – 11:00 → long
   - Profit at 9 EMA touch or HoD
   - Stop = loss of 20 EMA

### Psychology
- Clinical psychologists on staff: **Mike, Craig, Dr. Kenneth Reid**
- Meditation + mindfulness as routine
- 10 lessons including: develop confidence, know strengths/weaknesses,
  no two traders alike, accept change, manage stress, take breaks, build routine

### Trading book ("Fachbuch") — every trader must build own
- Stock selection criteria
- Indicator setup
- Entry rules
- Exit rules (TP + stop-loss)
- Psychology notes
- Trade examples
- Practice on simulator months before going live
- Available at bearbulltraders.com/gifts
