## Aziz's "11 Rules of Day Trading" (referenced in several of these videos)

Surfaces repeatedly in videos #2, #7, #8, #13 and in *How to Day Trade
for a Living* chapter 9.

1. Day trading is **not a strategy to get rich quick**.
2. Day trading is **not easy** — it's a serious business.
3. Day trading **does not mean constantly buying and selling stocks** —
   it means trading **stocks of the day**.
4. Pick a **proper online broker** and trading platform.
5. **Risk management** rules trump everything else.
6. Build a **detailed trading plan before the open**, every day.
7. The **best day traders rarely trade** — they wait for setups.
8. **Discipline** is the single most important quality.
9. **Be independent** — do not rely on chatroom calls blindly.
10. **Keep a trading journal** — review weekly.
11. **Take a break** if you're emotional, tired or on tilt.
